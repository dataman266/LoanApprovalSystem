## Key Concepts Explained Simply

### Risk Score (0-100)

Think of it like a grade:
- **0-25:** A+ (We love lending to you!)
- **25-50:** B (You're a good borrower)
- **50-75:** C (We have some concerns)
- **75-100:** F (Too risky)

### Debt-to-Income Ratio

Simple version: **"How much of your paycheck goes to debt?"**

Example:
```
Your paycheck: $5,000/month
Your debt payments: $1,000/month
Your Ratio: 20%

This means 20% of your money is already committed
to existing debts. We prefer under 36%.
```

### Credit Score

**What it measures:** Do you pay your bills on time?

- 700+ = Excellent (we like this!)
- 620-699 = Good (acceptable)
- Below 620 = We worry (harder to approve)
