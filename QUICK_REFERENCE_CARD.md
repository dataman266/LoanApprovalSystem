## 💡 Plain Language Tips

### Understanding Your Numbers

**Credit Score 710 vs 620:**
- 710 = ✅ Excellent (we want this)
- 620 = ⚠️ Risky (we worry)

**DTI 22% vs 50%:**
- 22% = ✅ Good (room for payment)
- 50% = ⚠️ Stretched (too much debt)

**Risk Score 35 vs 80:**
- 0-25 = ✅ Excellent
- 25-50 = ✅ Good
- 50-75 = ⚠️ Risky
- 75-100 = ❌ Very Risky
